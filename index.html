Self-study

Self-study has been one of the most important parts of my high school experience. It allowed me to go beyond school requirements and pursue questions that genuinely interested me.

Independent Research on Taiwan's Fertility Decline

I explored Taiwan's fertility decline through a time-series econometric approach, examining how economic and social factors may be associated with changes in birth patterns.

Research · Econometrics

Finance and ETF Research

I studied passive investing, ETF inclusion, valuation mismatch, and how market mechanisms may influence stock prices beyond company fundamentals.

Finance · Market Analysis

Company and Platform Strategy Analysis

What This Journey Means to Me

From Achievement to Inquiry

During high school, I gradually learned that academic growth is not only about completing tasks or collecting achievements. Research, debate, competitions, and self-study taught me that meaningful learning begins with asking better questions.

My interests may seem broad at first: economics, finance, mathematics, language, writing, and public speaking. However, they are connected by one central goal: I want to use analysis and communication to understand the world more deeply.
